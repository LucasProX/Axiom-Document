# 1.1 Register

> Goal: Register an account at [axiomcode.top](https://axiomcode.top) and complete email activation. This is the first step to using AxiomCode.

## Step 1: Open the registration page

Visit **[https://axiomcode.top](https://axiomcode.top)** and click the "Register" button in the top-right corner.

If your nickname / avatar is already shown in the top-right corner, you are already logged in and can skip straight to [Create an API key](/en/guide/apikey/enter).

![Open the registration page](/images/prepare/01-open-register.png)

## Step 2: Fill in your registration details

Complete the form field by field:

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Email** | Used for login, password recovery, and account notifications. Use a regular email address that can reliably receive mail |
| **Password** | At least 8 characters; a mix of letters + numbers + symbols is recommended |
| **Invitation code** | Optional |
| **Verification code** | Complete as prompted |

![Fill in your registration details](/images/prepare/02-fill-form.png)

::: warning Your email matters
Account security alerts, balance changes, and key-leak warnings are all sent to this email, so be sure to enter a real, regularly used address.
:::

## Step 3: Submit and complete activation

After clicking Register, check your inbox for the AxiomCode activation email (if you don't see it, check your "Spam / Promotions" folder), and click the activation link in the email to complete activation.

![Submit registration](/images/prepare/03-submit.png)

## Step 4: Log in and verify your account

Return to [axiomcode.top](https://axiomcode.top) and log in with the email and password you just used. After a successful login, your avatar / nickname appears in the top-right corner, and the left-hand menu shows entries such as Account, Billing, and API Keys.

![Interface after login](/images/prepare/05-after-login.png)

## FAQ

**Getting "This email is already registered"?** It means this email was registered before. Use "Recover password" or register with a different email.

**Not receiving the activation email?**

1. Check your "Spam / Promotions" folder.
2. Switch to Gmail / Outlook / 163 / a corporate mailbox; QQ Mail occasionally blocks messages.
3. If you still don't receive it, click "Resend activation email" on the login page.

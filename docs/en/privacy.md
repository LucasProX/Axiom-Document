# Privacy Policy

::: warning Draft
This policy is a draft. The content will be refined later; please refer to the officially published version.
:::

## Information We Collect

- **Account information**: registration email and login credentials.
- **Billing and usage metadata**: timestamps, token usage, status codes, models used, and so on.

## How We Use It

To provide and maintain the service, handle billing and settlement, ensure security, and troubleshoot issues.

## About Request Content

We retain only the **metadata** used for billing and do not persist your request bodies (prompts / response content). See the [FAQ](/en/guide/faq) for details.

## Third Parties

- Payments and top-ups are handled by the respective payment providers.
- Third-party logins such as GitHub / Google are handled by the corresponding platforms.

## Data Security

We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect data, but no transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be absolutely secure.

## Your Rights

You can view usage and manage keys in the console; to delete your account or export your data, please reach out through [Contact Us](/en/contact).

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, please see [Contact Us](/en/contact).
